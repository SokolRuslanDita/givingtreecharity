Our story

When Grandma Evelyn used to teach us about money she would say, "Spend a little, save a little and give a little away." Here at The GivingTREE, we believe in the power of collective giving and the impact of those small actions. Founded in 2017, we've been on a mission to change the world one micro-donation at a time. Our community of compassionate members has come together to "give a little away" to so many amazing charitable organizations.
